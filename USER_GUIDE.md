## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open the app in your mobile browser
2. Tap the **"Log in with Replit"** button
3. Enter your Replit account credentials
4. You'll be taken to the main Order screen

**[Screenshot: Login screen]**

### Understanding the Navigation

The app has tabs at the bottom of the screen:
- **Order** - Create new orders by searching products
- **Import** - Paste order text to quickly add items
- **Orders** - View and track your submitted orders

**[Screenshot: Bottom navigation tabs]**

## Creating Orders

### Step 1: Select a Brand

1. Tap the **Brand** dropdown at the top of the Order tab
2. Choose the brand you want to order from (Tynor, Morison, Biostige, etc.)
3. The product list will update to show only that brand's products

**[Screenshot: Brand dropdown selection]**

> **Important:** Each order can only contain products from ONE brand. You cannot mix brands in the same order.

### Step 2: Search for Products

1. Tap the **Search** box
2. Type the product name, SKU, or alias
3. Products matching your search will appear below

**[Screenshot: Product search with results]**

### Step 3: Add Products to Cart

For each product you want to order:

1. Find the product in the list
2. Use the **Qty** buttons (- and +) to set the quantity
3. If offering free items, use the **Free** buttons to set the free quantity
4. Tap the **cart icon** button to add to cart

**[Screenshot: Product card with Qty/Free controls highlighted]**

The cart icon in the header shows how many items are in your cart.

### Step 4: Review Your Cart

1. Tap the **cart icon** in the top-right corner
2. The cart drawer opens showing all your items
3. Review quantities and prices
4. You can adjust quantities or remove items here

**[Screenshot: Cart drawer with items]**

### Step 5: Enter Party Name and Place Order

1. In the cart, enter the **Party Name** (customer/retailer name) - this is required
2. Optionally add a **Delivery Address**
3. Tap **Place Order**
4. You'll see a success message and the cart will clear

**[Screenshot: Cart with Party Name field and Place Order button]**

## Using the Import Feature

The Import feature lets you quickly create orders by pasting text (like from a message or note).

### Step 1: Go to Import Tab

Tap the **Import** tab at the bottom of the screen.

**[Screenshot: Import tab selected]**

### Step 2: Paste Your Order Text

1. Tap the large text area
2. Paste or type your order text
3. The app understands formats like:
   - `Tovex 10` (product name and quantity)
   - `2x Knee Support` (quantity and product)
   - `Collagen Type II - 5 pcs` (various formats work)

**[Screenshot: Import text area with sample order text]**

### Step 3: Review Matched Products

1. Tap **Match Products**
2. The app shows which products it found matching your text
3. Green checkmarks mean a match was found
4. Red X means no match - you may need to search manually

**[Screenshot: Matched products list with quantities]**

### Step 4: Add to Cart

1. Review the matched items and quantities
2. Tap **Add to Cart**
3. The matched products are added to your cart
4. Go to the Order tab to complete your order

## Viewing Your Orders

### Order List

1. Tap the **Orders** tab
2. You'll see all your orders with:
   - Party name
   - Order date
   - Status badge (Created, Approved, Invoiced, Dispatched, Delivered)
   - Order total

**[Screenshot: Orders list showing multiple orders]**

### Order Status Meanings

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Created** | Order submitted, awaiting approval |
| **Approved** | Order approved, being processed |
| **Invoiced** | Invoice generated |
| **Dispatched** | Order shipped |
| **Delivered** | Order received by customer |

### Viewing Order Details

1. Tap any order in the list
2. A dialog opens showing:
   - Complete item list with quantities and prices
   - Order status and dates
   - Delivery information (if available)

**[Screenshot: Order details dialog]**

### Editing or Deleting Orders

You can only modify orders that are in **Created** or **Approved** status:

1. Open the order details
2. Tap **Edit** to modify details
3. Tap **Delete** to remove the order (you'll be asked to confirm)

> **Note:** Once an order is Invoiced, Dispatched, or Delivered, it cannot be changed.

## Sharing Orders via WhatsApp

### Single Order

1. Open any order from the Orders tab
2. Tap the **WhatsApp** button (green icon)
3. WhatsApp opens with a pre-formatted message containing:
   - Party name
   - All items with quantities
   - Order total
4. Select the contact and send

**[Screenshot: WhatsApp button on order details]**

### Order Update Messages

When an order status changes (Invoiced, Dispatched, Delivered), you can share updates:

1. Open the order details
2. The WhatsApp message includes updated status information
3. Great for keeping customers informed

## For BrandAdmins

BrandAdmins have additional capabilities for their assigned brands.

### Viewing All Brand Orders

1. Go to the **Orders** tab
2. You'll see orders from all users for your assigned brands
3. Use filters to find specific orders

### Updating Order Status

1. Open an order
2. Tap **Edit**
3. You can update:
   - **Status** (from Approved to Invoiced only)
   - **Delivery Company** (Guided, Xmaple, or Elmeric - based on brand)
   - **Invoice Number**
   - **Invoice Date**
   - **Actual Order Value**

**[Screenshot: BrandAdmin edit dialog with delivery company dropdown]**

### Delivery Company by Brand

Different brands use different delivery companies:
- **Biostige**: Guided only
- **Elmeric**: Elmeric or Guided
- **Morison**: Xmaple or Elmeric
- **Other brands**: All options available

## Tips for Mobile Users

### Quick Tips

1. **Use landscape mode** for easier typing on small screens
2. **Bookmark the app** to your home screen for quick access
3. **Use Import** for large orders - faster than searching each product
4. **Check your cart** before placing orders to verify quantities

### Saving Time

- **Product aliases**: Many products have short names that work in search
  - "tovex" finds "TOVEX TAB"
  - Search by SKU number for exact matches
  
- **Cart persists**: Your cart stays saved even if you close the browser

### Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't find a product | Try searching by SKU or check the brand filter |
| Can't place order | Make sure Party Name is filled in |
| Order won't save | Check your internet connection and try again |
| Wrong quantities | Edit in the cart before placing order |
